# Analytics Model

Higher-level analytics models that process time-series and event data to detect anomalies, generate KPIs, and produce forecasts. These models transform raw operational data into actionable insights that support strategic planning, performance monitoring, and early warning detection.


**Industry Use Cases:**

- **Smart Cities & Infrastructure:** Energy consumption forecasting and Predictive maintenance of public infrastructure  
- **Manufacturing & Industrial IoT:** Equipment failure prediction  
- **Retail & E-commerce:** Sales forecasting by product/category  
- **Finance & Banking:** Fraud detection through anomaly modeling
